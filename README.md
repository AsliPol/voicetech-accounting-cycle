# 📊 End-to-End Accounting Cycle — VoiceTech Solutions Inc.

> A full-cycle accounting simulation for a voice AI & freelance talent marketplace company.  
> Built to demonstrate hands-on competency in AP/AR, bank reconciliation, journal entries, month-end close, cash flow forecasting, and internal controls.

---

## 🏢 About the Simulated Company

**VoiceTech Solutions Inc.** is a fictional voice AI and talent marketplace — modelled after the structure of companies like Voices.com. The company works with freelance voice actors, enterprise content clients, and AI voice technology partners.

---

## 📋 What's Included

### 1. Accounts Payable (AP)
- 25 vendor invoices across 3 months (voice actors, software subscriptions, studio rentals, marketing)
- Payment status tracking: Paid / Pending / Overdue
- AP ledger reconciled against vendor statements
- **Fraud controls:** Duplicate invoice detection, overpayment flags, missing invoice alerts

### 2. Accounts Receivable (AR)
- 22 client invoices tracked across enterprise and SMB clients
- Collection status monitoring with DSO calculation per client
- Aging report: 30 / 60 / 90 day buckets

### 3. Bank Reconciliation
- 3 monthly reconciliations (January, February, March)
- 4 timing differences and 2 bank errors identified and resolved
- All months reconciled to **$0.00 variance**

### 4. Journal Entries
- 40+ entries covering: revenue recognition, expense accruals, prepaid amortization, depreciation, payroll

### 5. Month-End Close
- Full close checklist completed for Q1
- Includes: reconciliations, accruals, subledger tie-outs, management review package

Excel dashboard covering:
- Monthly cash inflow vs. outflow (Jan–Mar actuals + Apr–Jun forecast)

## 🛡️ AP Automation & Internal Controls

| Control | Description |
|---|---|
| Duplicate invoice detection | Flags same vendor + same amount within 30 days |
| Overpayment check | Alerts when payment exceeds invoice total |
| Missing invoice alert | Highlights gaps in invoice numbering |
| Risk scoring | Colour-coded vendor risk dashboard (Green / Amber / Red) |
| Freelancer tracker | Voice actor fees by project with profit-per-project calculation |

## 🎤 Voices-Specific Module: Freelancer Payment Tracking
